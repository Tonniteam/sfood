Seafood

The HuaiKhwang Seafood was founded in 2018.

The first store opened in the HuaiKhwang area of Bangkok, specializing in Thai specialties and snacks. With the concept of delicious, fresh and exquisite cuisine, we have broken the inherent eating habits and made dining more casual.

We select fresh seafood caught in the deep sea of Thailand on the same day and have been thoroughly cleaned.
