Our Restaurant

we have special foods

HuaiKhwang Seafood Restaurant is established in 2018. Our First outlet is located at Ratchada Road near HuaiKhwang MRT station. Our seafood preparation and cooking is based on Hong Kong, Malaysia, Singapore and Thai flavour suitable for both local and overseas patrons.

We emphasise on serving our patron with fresh, delicious and quality seafood, which they can pick and choose from a wide range of live seafood stocks which are housed in over 30 fish tanks in our restaurant.

Seafood such as lobster, Alaska Crab, shellfish, clams and fish is procured live, selectively from reliable sources.

Our seafood is prepared by a group of very dedicated and professional cook led by International and local Celebrity Chef whose burning passion and pride is to serve patron with special, breakthrough seafood experience that is unique and first class, unlike others in the region.

Through our original yet innovative recipe right from preparation, flavouring, arrangement to serving, each dish is carefully designed to tease and entice every patron's palate for their utmost, unforgettable seafood satisfaction at HuaiKhwang Restaurant.

With our EXCELLENT SERVICE, FRESH SEAFOOD and VALUE FOR PATRON’S dollar, HuaiKhwang Restaurant welcomes you to an unforgettable, mouth-watering and satisfying seafood experience with us.
